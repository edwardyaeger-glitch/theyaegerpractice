The practice is small by design and accepts new clients primarily through referral from a current or former client, a treating clinician, or a trusted colleague. If you were referred, a brief note describing the referring source and, in a sentence or two, what you are looking to address is sufficient.

Inquiries are read personally. A response typically follows within two business days, and, when appropriate, a short, no-fee video consultation is offered to determine whether the practice is a suitable fit.
