Privacy Policy

The Yaeger Practice · Edward J. Yaeger, Jr., LMHC
Effective date: September 1, 2023 · Last updated: January 1, 2026
Important: This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information collected through our website. A separate Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA) governs how we handle your Protected Health Information (PHI) as a healthcare provider and will be provided to you at intake. In the event of a conflict, the HIPAA Notice controls for PHI.

1. Who We Are

The Yaeger Practice (also doing business as Yaeger Group) is a private mental health counseling practice owned and operated by Edward J. Yaeger, Jr., LMHC. The Practice also collaborates with affiliated clinicians and partner organizations, including Grey Matter Counseling & Consulting, to extend access where appropriate. Services are provided via telehealth, subject to applicable licensure. References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to The Yaeger Practice and, where context requires, its affiliated clinicians.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact information (name, email, phone) you submit via forms, email, or phone
  • Scheduling and intake information you provide when inquiring about services
  • Any content of messages, voicemails, or emails you send us

Information collected automatically

  • Standard web log data (IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL)
  • Pages visited, time on page, and other analytics collected via cookies or similar technologies

Protected Health Information (PHI)

PHI created or received in the course of providing clinical services is governed by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, not by this website policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and website
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and professional obligations
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of clients, staff, and the public

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your information for targeted advertising.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

5. Email, Text, and Electronic Communication

Standard email and SMS are not secure and are not appropriate for detailed clinical content. Please use email and text only to schedule, confirm, or reschedule appointments. For clinical matters, use the secure portal or phone call methods we designate at intake. If you contact us electronically, you consent to us replying through the same channel at a basic administrative level.

6. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Service providers who help operate our website, scheduling, billing, or telehealth (under confidentiality agreements)
  • As required by law, subpoena, court order, or mandatory reporting obligations
  • In the event of a sale, merger, or transfer of the practice (in which case clients will be notified)
  • With your written authorization

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect your information. Telehealth sessions use HIPAA-compliant platforms. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Clinical records are retained for the period required by applicable state law and regulations, including New York State law where it applies. Website inquiry information is retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or for legitimate recordkeeping purposes.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the information below. Clients have additional rights regarding their clinical records under HIPAA and New York State law, as described in our HIPAA Notice.

10. Children

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Services to minors require authorization from a parent or legal guardian.

11. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites (such as our Substack). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites; please review their policies directly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly. Material changes will be highlighted.
